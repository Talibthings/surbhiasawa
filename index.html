Surbhi is a compassionate person who values the power of kindness. Surbhi aims to be empathetic and non-judgmental as a Psychologist. Her take on therapy is to facilitate the exploration of self. She believes the goal is to make individuals radically accept themselves. She is committed to creating a safe and collaborative environment with each to help them develop and improve their quality of life. She believes that no experience can be distorted or denied and views the importance of unlearning as much in life as learning.

Surbhi's Counselling services cover:

  • Individual Counselling
  • Couples Counselling
  • Child & Adolescent Counselling
  • Relationship/Family Counselling
  • Health Psychology Services

For too long, psychotherapy clinics have remained sterile, unwelcoming, clinical environments, adding to the stigma that surrounds mental health care. We believe it doesn’t have to be this way. We view therapy as one of the greatest gifts you can give yourself. Whether you’re hoping to increase daily wellbeing or to reduce symptoms of severe mental illness, there is space for you here.

Pro bono therapy

Surbhi provides her services without any fee. She believes that everyone, regardless of their financial situation, deserves access to quality mental health care. If one has volition to help others on their mental health journey, they may choose to donate to her non-profit organization.
